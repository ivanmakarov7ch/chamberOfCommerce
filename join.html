Membership level selection

Here you need to choose of what type of level membership you want. Fees are collected every year which gives you some benefit.

There are four options: Non-profit ($100), Bronze ($200), Silver ($400), Gold ($1000).

The different levels have different perks. Things like directory listings, business training, social media posts, luncheon tickets, sponsorships with levels of advertising, etc.
